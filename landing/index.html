What are Genetic Algorithm?

Genetic algorithms are inspired by the process of natural evolution. They work by creating a population of possible solutions and improving them over generations using selection, crossover, and mutation. Over time, the best solutions survive and evolve toward optimal results.

How are Visualizer Works?

Our visualizer simulates how agents evolve step by step. Each generation improves based on fitness evaluation. Users can observe how solutions adapt and improve dynamically, making complex algorithms easier to understand.

Our Goal

Our goal is to simplify the learning of complex algorithms through interactive visualization. We aim to make abstract concepts intuitive, helping students and developers grasp evolutionary computation easily.
